Honest by design. The green metrics are genuinely measured — your pulse is extracted with the POS algorithm (a research-grade rPPG method that combines the red, green and blue channels to cancel motion and lighting noise) from skin-only pixels. The amber metrics are derived from that pulse with standard physiological formulas (directional, not clinical). Not shown yet: blood pressure, glucose, SpO₂, hemoglobin — those need trained AI models (Phase 2). We will never display a made-up medical number.
